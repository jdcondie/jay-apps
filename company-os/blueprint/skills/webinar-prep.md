# Webinar Prep

> **Trigger:** "Prep a webinar for [date]" or "Set up webinar collateral" or "Build webinar assets for..."
> **Purpose:** Generate all webinar collateral for a specific date. Outputs registration page copy, 5 nurture emails, 5 LinkedIn promo posts, and the in-webinar offer script, all with dates and links filled in.

---

## Input Required

- Webinar date and time (with timezone)
- Zoom link
- Calendly/Cal.com link for audit booking
- Number of audit spots available this week
- Any industry-specific angle (optional)

---

## Outputs

### 1. Registration Page Copy

**Headline:** What Every Business Owner Needs to Know About AI in 2026

**Subheadline:** Most business owners know AI matters. Almost none of them know what to actually do with it.

**What you'll walk away with:**
- The 5 AI use cases generating the most ROI right now
- A clear picture of where AI can save your business time and money
- A live demo: watch a working AI tool get built in under 10 minutes
- Honest answers, no sales pitch

**Who this is for:** Business owners/operators, 10-200 employees, feeling behind on AI.

**Session structure:** The Gap (10 min), 5 Use Cases (10 min), Live Demo (15 min), Q&A (10 min)

**Fields:** First name, email, business type (optional)

**Trust line:** No recording sold. No pitch deck. Just 45 minutes you can use this week.

### 2. Email Sequence (5 emails, all with dates calculated from webinar date)

| Email | When | Subject | Key element |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Immediate on registration | "You're in, here's the Zoom link" | Zoom link, calendar link, "think of your most repetitive process" |
| 2 | 1-2 days before | "Before [Day], one question" | "Are you using AI meaningfully?" Preview session. P.S. forward line. |
| 3 | Morning of | "Today at [TIME], your Zoom link" | Zoom link, what to have ready, "the part that changes how people see AI" |
| 4 | 1 hour before | "We start in 1 hour" | Link only. Brief. "Reply if tech issues." |
| 5 | Within 2 hours after | "Thanks for joining, your next step" | Recap, free audit offer, [X] spots, Calendly link prominent |

Email 5 is the most important. Every other email builds to it.

### 3. LinkedIn Promo Posts (5 posts with dates calculated)

| Post | When | Angle | Hook |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 10-14 days before | The Gap | "95% of businesses using AI are still just asking ChatGPT questions" |
| 2 | 7-10 days before | The Use Case | Voice agent story, $25/mo, zero missed calls |
| 3 | 4-6 days before | Live Demo Teaser | "Blank screen. No setup. I'll build it live." |
| 4 | 2-3 days before | ROI Math | 25 employees, 20% productivity = 5 new hires without salaries |
| 5 | 24-48 hours before | Last Chance | Short, direct, no new info, register link |

### 4. In-Webinar Offer Script (final 5-7 minutes)

Setup (30 sec) -> Offer (60 sec) -> Scarcity (20 sec) -> THE PAUSE (60 sec, critical) -> Re-anchor (if needed) -> Close (30 sec)

**The key moment:** After showing the link, STOP TALKING. Every word is friction. Let people book.

**Chat cadence:** Post link at offer start, after pause, and at close.

**Target:** 15-25% of live attendees book an audit.

---

## Post-Webinar Checklist (within 2 hours)

1. Send Email 5 with Calendly link
2. Reply personally to chat questioners who didn't book
3. Post on LinkedIn: one specific insight from the session, no pitch
4. Send post-webinar DMs to attendees who didn't book (Version A from outbound playbook)
5. Send DMs to people who engaged with promo posts but didn't register (Version B)

The window is hot for 24-48 hours. Move fast.

---

## Tracking

Log after every webinar: registrants, attendees (live), audit bookings from session, audit bookings from Email 5, conversion rate.

---

## Voice Rules

All output follows Jay's voice: direct, short paragraphs, contractions, specific numbers, no em dashes, no banned words. LinkedIn posts use no markdown/bold (platform doesn't render it).
