# Client Reporter

> **Trigger:** "Write a monthly report for [client]" or "Build an impact report" or "Generate this month's report"
> **Purpose:** Generate monthly impact reports for active retainer clients. Input: month's activities and metrics. Output: formatted report with executive summary, KPIs, activity log, and roadmap status.

---

## Input Required

- Client name and month
- Solutions deployed / automations built this month
- Hours saved (estimated or measured)
- Costs reduced or revenue recovered
- Team training sessions conducted
- AI Operator development progress
- Roadmap status updates
- Any notable wins or metrics

## Formatting Rules

- Output as .docx
- Navy (#1B2A4A) headers, orange (#E8541A) accent lines
- Tables with navy header rows (white text), light (#F5F0EB) alternating rows
- Keep it to 3-4 pages max
- Lead every section with the number, not the narrative
- Use their language for what was built, not technical jargon

---

## Rules

- The report should make the retainer feel like a no-brainer. Lead with value delivered.
- Cumulative value tracking is the most important section. It answers "is this worth it?" every month.
- Don't pad the report with activity that didn't produce value. Shorter and honest beats longer and fluffy.
- Always end with a clear next month preview so they know what's coming.
- Jay's voice: direct, specific numbers, short paragraphs, no em dashes, no banned words.
