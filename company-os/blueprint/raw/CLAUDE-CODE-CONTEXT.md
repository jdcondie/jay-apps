## Sales & Outreach

### Entry Paths

**Path A: Chamber of Commerce Seminar**
- Free 30-45 min AI tutorial for local business owners
- No pitch during the session
- Sign up interested attendees for a free AI audit at the end
- The audit is the second foot in the door

**Path B: Live Demo (MFM Method)**
- Get a meeting with a business owner
- Open Claude, prompt with their business context, generate 10 AI use cases
- Pick one, build it live in Replit in 10 minutes
- Let their reaction close the deal

**Path C: Cold Outreach**
- Scrape home services companies (already tested: 80 texts, 60 responses)
- 5-email cold sequence + text outreach + LinkedIn connections
- Goal: book 15-minute discovery calls

### Discovery Call Framework
- First call is discovery only. No solutioning.
- Open with sentiment: "Where are you personally with AI right now?"
- Map workflows: "What do you do 10-20 times a week that's repetitive?"
- Quantify pain: "How many hours a week? What's the hourly cost for that role?"
- Close with: "Let me synthesize what I heard and come back with options."
- Never demo, whiteboard, or promise outcomes on the discovery call.

### The "No Solution" Rule
If pressed for a solution during discovery: "That's a great question. There are 2-3 tools that could handle that, but I don't want to give you a generic answer. I want to test them against your specific workflow and come back with the right one in the report."

## Delivery Model

### Solution Levels
- **Level 1 — Native AI:** Using built-in AI features (ChatGPT, Claude) for immediate time savings. Start here always.
- **Level 2 — AI + Automation:** Connecting tools (Zapier, Make) with AI in the loop. 80% of value at a fraction of Level 3 cost.
- **Level 3 — Custom Systems:** Vibe-coded apps, custom agents, full workflow orchestration. Only when Levels 1-2 are insufficient.

### Retainer Framework: "The AI Operating System"
4-week recurring cadence:
1. **Week 1: Executive Alignment** — CEO strategy session, roadmap review
2. **Week 2: Department Activation** — Team-specific AI implementation
3. **Week 3: Implementation Sprint** — Build and deploy new automations
4. **Week 4: Training + Reporting** — Team training, monthly impact report

### AI Operator Concept
Identify an internal champion inside the client's org. Curious, high-agency employee. Train them to run discovery conversations, capture AI opportunities using Solution Briefs, and reduce dependency on me over time. A coach who trained their team is harder to fire than a consultant who built a tool.

### Universal Use Cases for Home Services
- **24/7 Voice Agent:** Answers phone, books appointments, handles FAQs
- **AI Lead Follow-Up:** Instant text/email response, qualification, booking
- **Estimate Follow-Up Sequence:** 3-touch automated sequence on every open quote
- **Smart Scheduling:** Online booking, reminders, rescheduling
- **Owner Dashboard:** One screen showing leads, revenue, team output, pipeline
- **Review Collection:** Automated post-job review requests
- **Seasonal Reactivation:** AI campaigns to re-engage lapsed customers
