# Brand Voice

## Tone

Direct, no fluff, no jargon. Speak like a peer operator who's done this 100 times, not a consultant reading from a slide deck. Lead with the problem, then the solution. Use specific numbers over vague claims. Short paragraphs. Contractions always.

## We Sound Like

- A senior operator sharing what actually works, not theory
- Someone who's built this for dozens of companies and is giving you the shortcut
- A straight-talking friend who also happens to know exactly how to fix your business

## We Never Sound Like

- A generic AI consultant delivering buzzwords and PDFs
- A corporate vendor deck with "synergy" and "digital transformation"
- A tutor explaining basics to a student
- An overly cautious advisor hedging every statement

## Words We Use

| Use This | Not This |
|----------|----------|
| build | implement |
| system | solution |
| tested | evaluated |
| broke | underperformed |
| fix | remediate |
| run | execute |
| set up | deploy |
| works | performs |

## Words We Never Use

delve, realm, harness, unlock, tapestry, paradigm, cutting-edge, revolutionize, landscape, leverage, synergy, innovative, game-changer, seamless, optimize, scalable, robust, breakthrough, empower, streamline, elevate, transformative, frictionless, utilize, supercharge, comprehensive, holistic, turnkey, next-gen, AI-powered, future-proof, data-driven

## Writing Rules

- No em dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses.
- Short paragraphs. 1-2 sentences default, 3 max.
- Contractions always. "Don't," not "do not."
- Specific numbers over vague ranges.
- Active voice always.
- Numbers as digits.
- Bold sparingly, 1-2 key moments per section.
- No walls of text.
- Lead with the answer, not the reasoning.

## Example (Before / After)

**Before (generic):** "We leverage cutting-edge AI solutions to streamline your go-to-market operations and unlock unprecedented growth potential."

**After (our voice):** "We install AI systems that answer your phones, follow up on every lead, and recover the revenue you're losing to slow response times. Most home services companies leave $5K-$15K/month on the table. We fix that."

## Voice in Action (Real Examples)

**Cold email opener:**
> "I was looking at [Company Name] and noticed you're running Google Ads but after-hours calls go to voicemail. Most [industry] companies lose 20-30% of their leads just because nobody picks up after 5pm."

**LinkedIn post hook:**
> "95% of businesses using AI are still just asking ChatGPT questions and reading the answers. That's not a strategy. That's a fancy search engine."

**Sales call close:**
> "Based on what you've shared, I think there's a strong fit here. The way we work is as your fractional Chief AI Officer. We come in on a monthly basis, handle the strategy, build out the automations, and train your team. Depending on the size and scope, that's typically $10,000 to $15,000 a month."

**Objection response:**
> "One person costs $6K/mo. AI costs $25/mo, works 24/7, never quits. Most of what you'd hire for can be automated at a fraction of the cost."

## Formatting by Context

| Context | Adjustment |
|---|---|
| Cold email | Under 120 words. No links in body. Plain text. Mobile-first. |
| LinkedIn post | No markdown/bold (platform doesn't render). Short paragraphs. Hook before "see more" cut. Hashtags at end (3-4 max). |
| Client proposal (.docx) | Navy/orange brand system. Tables for data. Bracketed [fill-in fields]. 2-3 pages max. |
| Internal reference (.md) | Can be longer. Tables, headers, code blocks OK. Still short paragraphs. |
| Sales call script | Conversational. Natural. Not word-for-word. Pauses and silence are fine. |
