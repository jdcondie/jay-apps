# Company Overview

## What We Sell

AI implementation for home services companies (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, cleaning). We don't sell software. We sell a transformation.

We install AI systems that capture leads, automate follow-ups, and eliminate manual admin work. The core framework is STE:

1. **Strategy** — How AI affects their industry, competitors, and business model. Positions the CEO to stay ahead.
2. **Transformation** — Building and automating processes with AI. Dashboards, automations, CRM integrations, AI agents.
3. **Education** — Training the CEO and every employee to use AI daily. Target: 20% productivity gain across all staff.

### Solution Levels
- **Level 1 (Native AI):** Using built-in AI features (ChatGPT, Claude) for immediate time savings. Start here always.
- **Level 2 (AI + Automation):** Connecting tools (Zapier, Make) with AI in the loop. 80% of value at a fraction of Level 3 cost.
- **Level 3 (Custom Systems):** Vibe-coded apps, custom agents, full workflow orchestration. Only when Levels 1-2 are insufficient.

### Universal Use Cases for Home Services
- 24/7 Voice Agent: Answers phone, books appointments, handles FAQs
- AI Lead Follow-Up: Instant text/email response, qualification, booking
- Estimate Follow-Up Sequence: 3-touch automated sequence on every open quote
- Smart Scheduling: Online booking, reminders, rescheduling
- Owner Dashboard: One screen showing leads, revenue, team output, pipeline
- Review Collection: Automated post-job review requests
- Seasonal Reactivation: AI campaigns to re-engage lapsed customers
