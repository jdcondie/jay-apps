# Outbound Playbook

## Overview

Three entry paths into the sales pipeline: Chamber of Commerce seminars (free AI tutorial, sign up for audit), live demos using the MFM method (build an AI tool live in the meeting), and cold outreach (email + text + LinkedIn). All paths funnel into a free AI efficiency audit, which is the real foot in the door.

## ICP Summary

- **Target companies:** Home services (HVAC, plumbing, electrical, landscaping, cleaning), $1M-$20M revenue, 5-50 employees, Salt Lake City metro
- **Target buyers:** Owner/CEO, operations manager
- **Key signals:** No online booking, slow response times (test by calling after hours), manual scheduling, no CRM, growing but disorganized
- **Qualifying criteria:** Has a real website, 10+ Google reviews, owner name findable, appears to be doing $1M+
- **Disqualify:** Already has AI/tech team, under $1M, franchise HQ (not individual operators)

### Message Priority Matrix

| Situation | Best message | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Cold connection, unknown industry | Connection A + Follow-up A | Broad, relevant, clear ask |
| Cold connection, known pain point | Connection B + Follow-up B | Pain-led, faster to audit |
| Local business owner | Connection C + Follow-up A | Community angle, then value |
| Webinar attendee, no booking | Post-webinar A | Specific, low pressure |
| Engaged with content, no registration | Post-webinar B | Acknowledges their action |
| Warm contact, slow to respond | Post-webinar C | Personal, story-based |

### Sending Rules

- **Connection request to follow-up:** Wait for acceptance, then send within 24-48 hours. Don't wait a week.
- **If they respond but aren't ready:** Say "Makes sense, I'll check back in a month" and follow through.
- **If they say "not interested":** Reply "Totally fair, thanks for letting me know" and move on. Never argue.
- **Rotate versions.** Don't send the same message to everyone in the same industry.
- **Response tracking:** Simple spreadsheet. Name, date connected, message sent, response, outcome. Review weekly.

### LinkedIn Posting Rules

- When someone comments "interested" or asks a question, reply and drop the link directly.
- Don't delete posts that underperform. Let them run 48-72 hours minimum.

## Live Demo (MFM Method)

### Structure (15-20 minutes)

**Phase 1: The Setup (5 min)**
Get them talking about their pain. "What's the most time-consuming thing in your business right now?"

**Phase 2: The Prompt (3 min)**
Open Claude. Type: "I'm a [their business type]. This is my business. Give me 10 ways I should be using AI."

**Phase 3: The Build (7 min)**
Pick one output. Prompt: "Give me the prompt to build use case #1." Throw it into Replit. Show them the working tool.

**Phase 4: The Close (5 min)**
"Can we go deeper? I do a free audit where I map the top 2-3 opportunities for your specific business." Have proposal ready to send same day.

**Key insight:** Their reaction tells you everything. If they lean forward, you have a deal.

### In-Webinar Offer Script (Final 5-7 minutes)

**The Setup (30 sec):**
> "Before I let everyone go, I want to offer something to everyone on today's call. This is the most valuable thing I'm going to say in the next 5 minutes, so stay with me."

**The Offer (60 sec):**
> "I'm setting aside time this week for free 30-minute AI audits. You and I get on a call. **The Pause (60 seconds, CRITICAL):**
Stop talking. Let people pull out their phones. Let them open the link. Let them book. Every word after showing the link is friction.

**The Re-Anchor (if needed):**
> "The businesses getting the most out of AI right now are not the most technical ones. They're the ones who got clear on their highest-value use case and moved first. That's what the audit does."

**The Close (30 sec):**
> "The link is in the chat. Book directly. I'll confirm within a few hours. For anyone who can't make it this week, reply to the email you'll get from me in the next hour."

**Target:** 15-25% of live attendees book an audit. If below 10%, the issue is the pause. Stay quiet.

## 30-Day Launch Sequence Reference

**Days 1-7:** Build capability + infrastructure (tools, website, target list, seminar booking)
**Days 8-14:** Get in rooms (LinkedIn outreach, seminar prep, book first calls)
**Days 15-21:** First close (run seminar, discovery calls, present proposal)
**Days 22-30:** Momentum + niche signal (deliver, ask for referral, identify niche pattern)

**90-day goal:** 3-5 retainer clients at $10-15K/month = $30-75K MRR

**Non-negotiables:**
- Do not build an LLC or website before you have a paying client
- Seminar = volume, LinkedIn = precision. Run both in parallel.
- Niche down only after you have signal. Don't pick a niche from theory.
- Every call ends with a scheduled next step.
