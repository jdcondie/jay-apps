# GTM Skills

These are 5 starter skills for go-to-market workflows. They're templates, not finished skills.

## How to use them

1. Read through each skill to understand the frameworks
2. Tell Claude: "Read this skill and customize it for my business. Interview me about what needs to change."
3. Claude will ask about your ICP, voice, tools, and processes, then rewrite the skill
4. Once customized, move the skill to your Company OS skills/ folder:

```
cp gtm-skills/outbound-copywriter.md your-company-os/skills/
```

Or ask Claude: "Move the customized skills into my Company OS skills folder."

## The 5 skills

- **outbound-copywriter** - Cold email sequences (SPARK framework, 7 power patterns, Chris Voss techniques)
- **linkedin-post-writer** - LinkedIn posts in your voice (voice calibration, 7 frameworks, 12 hooks)
- **icp-modeller** - Ideal customer profile (3-dimension scoring, tier system, validation)
- **gtm-strategist** - GTM motion design (5-layer stack, channel strategy, budget allocation)
- **discovery-prep** - Pre-call research briefs (4-layer research, objection handling, call playbooks)

## After customizing

Delete this guide.md file and the original templates. Your skills/ folder in your Company OS is the source of truth.
